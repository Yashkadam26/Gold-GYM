THE LEGACY OF IRON

A journey from a single basement to a global fitness empire.

1990

The Iron Garage

Founded in a dusty garage in Mumbai. Our founder, driven by pure passion, hand-welded the first squat rack. We started with 5 members and a dream to change Indian fitness forever.

1998

Professional Arena

We moved from the garage to our first 5,000 sq. ft. facility. This year marked our partnership with international bodybuilding legends.

2005

The Franchise Boom

Gold Gym becomes a household name. We opened our 20th center, introducing world-class cardio equipment and certified personal training to the middle class.

2012

50 Cities Strong

From Mumbai to Delhi, Gold Gym became the standard for fitness in India. We introduced the first 'Group X' classes including Zumba and HIIT.

2015

Digital Integration

We launched our first mobile app. No more paper workout logs—members started tracking their progress digitally, setting the stage for a data-driven fitness era.

2020

The Virtual Pivot

When the world stopped, we didn't. We launched 'Gold Gym at Home,' reaching over 50,000 users through live-streamed sessions daily.

2022

Hybrid Fitness

Survival of the fittest. We pivoted to hybrid models, offering home-workout streaming alongside our elite physical floor space to keep India moving during tough times.

2026

AI & Bio-Hacking

Today. With AI-integrated machines and recovery bio-pods, we aren't just a gym; we are a human performance lab. The legacy continues with you.
